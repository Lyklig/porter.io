The Minimal Viable Website

The first website. The business model prover.

You'll need this if:

  • You need to prove your new business model
  • You need to create brand awareness for your business
  • You need a way for potential leads to contact you
  • You need to automate a few steps of your sales process

This will consists of:

  • A beautifully designed website or landing page
  • A contact form for users to reach out to you
  • Analytics installed to track the performance of your goals

What will happen throughout the process:

  1. We'll talk about your needs and potential solutions
  2. You'll gather all content needed for your website, that includes photos and text
  3. I'll create a couple looks for your website, a conservative and a liberal look
  4. We'll choose between the two looks and build out the final site

Scope

What's Included
  • 1-2 page website
  • Contact form to capture leads
  • Web analytics to monitor KPIs
What's Extra
  • Content Strategy, writing and photography
  • Branding such as logos, business cards
  • Features needing a database such as user accounts
  • Content management system for editing content without code
  • More than 2 content-based pages. Shopify-generated pages such as cart are included. Ask for more details.

The Minimal Viable Product

The first app design. Everything takes baby steps.

You'll need this if:

  • You need to prove your product has market fit
  • You need to automate a few steps of your business

This will consists of:

  • An app designed for a couple core features
  • An app on one platform

What will happen throughout the process:

  1. We'll talk about your needs, KPIs and potential solutions
  2. We'll research your potential users and figure out how they current handle the problem you're trying to solve
  3. We'll create flow charts to figure out how your users will utilize your app
  4. We'll create prototypes and test the design to find gaps to fill
  5. We'll hand off those designs to the development team to begin the development process
  6. We'll ship the iteration, collect feedback and make iterations to the design

System Optimization

Let's create a better way for you to handle your business.

You'll need this if:

  • You whole flow or process is in total chaos
  • You're constantly misplacing emails & notes
  • Your team is late on projects often
  • You have a hard time getting things done
  • Your current process has so many steps

This will provide you with:

  • A system of apps that will manage your tasks and projects

What will happen throughout the process:

  1. We'll talk about the problems you're currently having with your system
  2. Figure out your ideal productivity or business life
  3. List out the current apps you're using and figure out if they're still needed
  4. Audit all of the types of data you utilize in your system
  5. Create a new flow that will make organizing your data simpler
  6. Training to help you get accustom to your new system
